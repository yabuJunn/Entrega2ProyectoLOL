DREAM DRAGON YASUO

Yasuo, a fearless Ionian with unbreakable willpower, is also a skilled swordsman capable of controlling the wind and using it against his enemies. As a proud young man, he was unjustly accused of murdering his master. Unable to prove his innocence, he was forced to kill his own brother in self-defense.

TRANQUILITY DRAGON KARMA

No mortal exemplifies the spiritual traditions of Ionia more than Karma. She is the living embodiment of an ancient soul reincarnated countless times, carrying all her accumulated memories into each new life, and blessed with power that few can comprehend.

GUARDIAN OF THE SANDS

Guardians of the Sand is a series of alternate future/universe skins in League of Legends. Set in an alternate Shurima, all of the champions are tomb raiders, ancient tomb guardians, and evil spirits of the desert

SPIRIT BLOSSOM YONE

In life, he was Yone—half-brother of Yasuo, and renowned student of his village's sword school. But upon his death at the hands of his brother, he found himself hunted by a malevolent entity of the spirit realm, and was forced to slay it with its own sword.

SPIRIT BLOSSOM AHRI

The famed Spirit of Salvation, and the fox all mortals are beckoned towards when their souls arrive to the spirit realm. A capricious, whimsical spirit who sees the fate of the living as a game of chase, she offers the chance for souls to find their final rest…
